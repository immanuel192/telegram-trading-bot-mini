Some of patterns that I observed
- symbol is always XAUUSD
- isImmediate is always true, always market order

Enter trade pattern
- Usually they will enter 2 trades in 2 different messages, come in one after another, and usually after a few secs. 
- They are also enter multiple orders together (NOT sure if that is DCA). They mention order in quotedMessage so that we know which one is which.
- When they say "Gold buy now" or "Gold sell now", that will be the first trade => we will classify as command = LONG/SHORT, entry = null, stopLoss = null, isLinkedWithPrevious=false. For this type of command, we dont take the quotedMessage , quotedFirstMessage and prevMessage into account.
- Then come with the next message usually in this format "💥GOLD Buy 4091- 4089\n\n✅TP  4094\n✅TP  4111\n\n💢SL  4086" or "💥GOLD Sell 4039- 4041\n\n✅TP  4036\n✅TP  4019\n\n💢SL  4044"  => this will be 2nd trade that should link with previous one that is in prevMessage. For this type of message, entry = null as well (since all market trade), isLinkedWithPrevious = true, and extract takeProfits as well as stopLoss

General information
- "@Gold +150 Pips running ✅🚀" => just to inform that the trade is running
- "GOLD - TP HIT 30 +Pips ✅✅" => Same, to inform TP hit
- "ready signal"
